    How to Use StopwatchPro

    StopwatchPro is designed to be simple enough that you never need instructions — but here's exactly how each feature works if you want to get the most out of it.

    1. Start timing: Click the Start button or press the Space bar to begin counting. The display updates in real time down to the millisecond.
    2. Record a lap: While the stopwatch is running, click Lap or press "L" to log a split. Each lap shows both its individual time and the running total.
