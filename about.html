About

Built for anyone who needs to time anything.

What is StopwatchPro?

StopwatchPro is a free, browser-based stopwatch and lap timer. There's nothing to download, no account to create, and no fees. Just open the page and start timing — whether you're on a phone, tablet, or desktop computer.

Why we built it

Most online stopwatches are cluttered, slow to load, or riddled with pop-ups. We built StopwatchPro to be the opposite: fast, clean, and reliable. It works the moment you open it, even on a slow connection.

Who uses StopwatchPro?

  • Athletes and coaches tracking workout splits
  • Students using the Pomodoro technique
  • Cooks timing recipes to the second
  • Teachers managing classroom activities
  • Scientists and researchers recording experiments
  • Anyone who needs a quick, accurate timer

Features

  • Millisecond-accurate timing
  • Unlimited lap recording with best/worst split highlights
  • Keyboard shortcuts (Space, L, R)
  • Works on all devices and browsers
  • No ads that interrupt timing
  • 100% free, forever
