How to Use StopwatchPro

Quick starter manual to help you master our online timer utilities.

⏱ Online Stopwatch

  • Start / Stop: Click the main primary action button or press the Spacebar on your keyboard.
  • Lap / Split Times: While the stopwatch is running, click "Lap" or press L to record split intervals. The list tracking your fastest and slowest laps appears right below the main clock interface.
  • Resetting: Stop the clock and click "Reset" to wipe the session back to zero.

⏳ Countdown Timer

  • Setting a Duration: Input your target hours, minutes, and seconds directly into the configuration rows, or use pre-configured rapid-presets.
  • Custom Alarms: Use the drop-down selectors to sample and choose alternative ring tones.
  • Presets Library: Frequently repeated routines can be saved locally to your profile for rapid setup on future site visits.

🔄 Interval Timer

  • Build a Routine: Establish your activity block duration, short cooldown rest phases, and designated loop cycles.
  • Audio Cues: Distinct alert sounds notify you the exact second a sequence segment concludes.

♟ Chess Clock

  • Set Time Controls: Assign baseline clock limits for both match participants.
  • Turn Alternations: Tap or click your side of the screen to lock your countdown progress and simultaneously initiate your opponent's match clock.
